PrivateX Benefits

01

Sanction Proof Smart Contracts

PrivateX developed Sanction Proof private smart contracts which automatically changes every 10 days so no Government or any entity can Sanction a particular address. This enables users to use the platform privately and anonymously without the threat of sanction and tyranny.

02

ETH Rewards

PrivateX will share 10% of the fees generated by the platform to the token holders in ETHER . Depending on the amount of tokens in the wallet, rewards are distributed.

03

USDT Rewards

PrivateX will share 10% of the fees from the swap to the token holders in USDT. Depending on the amount of tokens in the wallet, rewards are distributed.

04

NFT Marketplace Revenue Sharing

PrivateX will share the fees 10% of the fees from the NFT Marketplace sale to the token holders in ETHER . Depending on the amount of tokens in the wallet, rewards are distributed.

05

Staking Rewards

Stake PRVT and earn rewards in PRVT tokens. To stake, you dont need any particular hardware or device. You can do it directly from your phone or PC. Stake PRVT to passively make money from your tokens.

06

Decentralized Governance

PrivateX DAO allows token holders a privilege to control the direction and innovation of the platform without a controlling authority.
